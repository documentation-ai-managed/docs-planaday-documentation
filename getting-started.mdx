## Account Setup

Start your Planaday journey by creating an account and configuring your initial settings. You'll need to provide basic information about your organization and preferred settings to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit planaday.com and click "Start Free Trial." Enter your email address and create a secure password. You'll receive a confirmation email with a verification link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Click the verification link in your email to activate your account. This step ensures the security of your account and allows us to send important updates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile" icon="settings">
    Fill in your organization details, including name, type (school, training center, etc.), and contact information. This helps us customize your experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Preferences" icon="sliders">
    Configure time zone, date formats, and notification settings. Choose whether to enable two-factor authentication for enhanced security.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Enrollment Settings

Determine how students can join your course and set any prerequisites or restrictions. This helps manage course capacity and ensures appropriate student placement.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Open Enrollment" icon="unlock">
    Allow anyone to enroll without restrictions. This is ideal for public courses or introductory sessions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Restricted Access" icon="lock">
    Require approval or prerequisites before enrollment. Perfect for advanced courses or certification programs.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Adding Students

Populate your course with student information to begin tracking progress and managing communications. You can add students individually or import data in bulk.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Individual Entry" icon="user-plus">
    Click "Add Student" and enter details like name, email, and enrollment date. You can also upload a profile photo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bulk Import" icon="upload">
    Use our CSV import feature for large groups. Download the template, fill in student data, and upload to quickly populate your course.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Information" icon="check-circle">
    Review imported data for accuracy. Make corrections as needed before finalizing the import.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
  Student data is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with GDPR and FERPA regulations to protect personal information.
</Callout>

## Dashboard Overview

Familiarize yourself with the main dashboard to monitor course activity and access key features. The dashboard provides real-time insights into enrollment and progress.

<Expandable title="Dashboard Components" default-open="true">
